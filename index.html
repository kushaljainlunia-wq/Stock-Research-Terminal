📊 Fundamental Stock Analysis Dashboard

A data-driven equity research project focused on analyzing listed Indian companies through financial statement analysis, valuation metrics, growth indicators, profitability assessment, risk evaluation, and business fundamentals.

About This Project

Welcome to my Fundamental Stock Analysis Dashboard, a project developed to perform structured and comprehensive analysis of listed Indian companies.


The dashboard integrates financial statement analysis, valuation metrics, profitability assessment, growth indicators, risk evaluation, and business fundamentals into an interactive analytical framework.


The objective of this project is to transform publicly available financial information into meaningful insights and establish a standardized approach for evaluating investment opportunities across industries.


Through this initiative, I aim to demonstrate the application of financial analysis, business understanding, data visualization, and analytical problem-solving in the field of equity research and investment analysis.

From Data to Decisions

Financial platforms and stock screeners excel at providing data. However, investment decisions require much more than numbers alone. Investors must understand business quality, management execution, competitive advantages, valuation, risks, and future growth prospects before building conviction.

This dashboard is designed as a research layer on top of financial data platforms. Rather than replacing stock screeners, it helps investors transform information into structured investment insights.

🔍 Recommended Workflow

The most effective approach is to use this dashboard alongside financial data platforms and stock screeners.

  1. Use stock screeners to discover and shortlist potential investment opportunities.
  2. Open the company dashboard to perform deeper analysis.
  3. Understand management quality, competitive positioning, risks, valuation, and investment thesis.
  4. Review bull, base, and bear case scenarios.
  5. Use the consolidated insights to support investment decisions.

Key Philosophy

Financial statements tell the story of the past. Investment decisions depend on understanding the future. This dashboard attempts to bridge that gap by combining financial analysis, business understanding, valuation assessment, risk evaluation, and investment reasoning into a single research framework.

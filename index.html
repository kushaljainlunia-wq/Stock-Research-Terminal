📊 Fundamental Stock Analysis Dashboard

A data-driven equity research project focused on analyzing listed Indian companies through financial statement analysis, valuation metrics, growth indicators, profitability assessment, risk evaluation, and business fundamentals.

About This Project

Welcome to my Fundamental Stock Analysis Dashboard, a project developed to perform structured and comprehensive analysis of listed Indian companies.


The dashboard integrates financial statement analysis, valuation metrics, profitability assessment, growth indicators, risk evaluation, and business fundamentals into an interactive analytical framework.


The objective of this project is to transform publicly available financial information into meaningful insights and establish a standardized approach for evaluating investment opportunities across industries.


Through this initiative, I aim to demonstrate the application of financial analysis, business understanding, data visualization, and analytical problem-solving in the field of equity research and investment analysis.

What This Dashboard Covers

📈 Financial Analysis

Revenue, margins, profitability and performance trends.

💰 Valuation

P/E, P/B, valuation indicators and market assessment.

⚖️ Risk Assessment

Business, industry and financial risk evaluation.

📊 Visualization

Interactive charts and investment insights.

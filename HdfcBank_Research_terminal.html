About the Business

HDFC Bank is India's largest private sector bank by assets and market cap, with a 15% market share in banking advances and 37% in private sector advances. Following its landmark merger with HDFC Limited (July 2023), the bank became a full-service financial conglomerate. Its network of 9,689 branches and 21,172 ATMs across 4,175 cities reinforces India's deepest retail banking franchise. The bank operates across retail banking, wholesale banking, treasury, insurance (HDFC Life, HDFC ERGO), asset management (HDFC AMC), and NBFC (HDB Financial Services).
Competitive Moat: Unmatched branch network density, dominant CASA franchise, superior underwriting culture, and subsidiary ecosystem create durable competitive advantages across every banking segment.
